PB and J Sandwhich

Description

The peanut butter and jelly sandwhich is a classic that is sure to please almost anyone who doesn't have a peanut allergy.

With simple, cheap ingredients, this sandwhich makes a great breakfast, lunch or even dinner!

Ingredients
  • Two slices of breaad
  • Jelly
  • Peanut butter
Steps
    Spread peanut butter on one slice of bread
    Spread jelly on the other slice of bread
    Put the two slices of bread together, mixing pb an jelly
    Enjoy!
